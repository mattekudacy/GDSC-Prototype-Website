GDSC Alumni Testomnonies

Not yet convinced? Here are the past alumni of GDSC UE



Cyrus C. Mante
Data Enginner Intern at Nextpay.ph
Former Mobile Development Lead

Because of Google Developer Student Clubs, I get to know more about the tech community. It gave me a lot of connections
with people that eventually helped me in getting my first internship at AIA Philippines as a Data Analytics Intern. I also had
a lot of friends that encouraged me in pursuing my professional career in Data Science. GDSC thought me some soft skills
like leadership and commitment towards your work. They also gave me recogniton from finishing their codelabs which I added
to my portfolio so recruiters knew that I am knowledgable in Google Technolgies. My position as Mobile Development Lead
increased my chance in getting accepted in jobs because of my experience in managing a team and also the technical skils
like coding and domain knowledge.
