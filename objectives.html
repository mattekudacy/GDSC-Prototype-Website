OBJECTIVES

Concept of GDSC

GDSC are community groups for students interested in Google developer tools who are in college or university. Students who want to learn more about programming are welcome to attend.

Why Join?

Students will learn development skills, use technology to solve problems, and be inspired to become world-class developers and changemakers.

Target Audience

University students and anyone, including faculty members, who desire to learn development skills and work on real-world challenges are encouraged to participate in GDSC events.
